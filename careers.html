Careers

Join Our Team!

At iBlackAI, we believe that great ideas can transform the world. We are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of creativity and technology, and we are always on the lookout for passionate and talented individuals to join our team. If you are excited about using cutting-edge technology to empower creativity and innovation, you might be the perfect fit for us!

Our Culture

We foster a culture of collaboration, creativity, and continuous learning. Our team is made up of diverse individuals who bring unique perspectives and skills to the table. We encourage open communication, embrace new ideas, and value each member's contributions. At IblackAI, you will find an inclusive environment where everyone is empowered to thrive.

Why Work With Us?

Innovative Environment: Be part of a team that is at the forefront of AI and image generation technology. Work on projects that challenge you and help you grow professionally.

Flexible Work Culture: We understand the importance of work-life balance. Our flexible work arrangements allow you to work in a way that suits your lifestyle.

Career Development: We are committed to your professional growth. We offer training, mentorship, and opportunities for advancement within the company.

Competitive Compensation: We provide a competitive salary and benefits package that reflects your skills and experience.

Current Openings

We are currently looking for talented individuals to fill the following positions:

1. AI/ML Engineer

Responsibilities:

  • Develop and optimize machine learning algorithms for image generation.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to integrate AI solutions into our platform.
  • Conduct research on the latest advancements in AI technology and implement improvements.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, AI, or a related field.
  • Experience with machine learning frameworks (e.g., TensorFlow, PyTorch).
  • Strong programming skills in Python or similar languages.
  • Knowledge of image processing techniques is a plus.

2. Frontend Developer

Responsibilities:

  • Design and implement user-friendly interfaces for our web application.
  • Collaborate with designers and backend developers to create a seamless user experience.
  • Optimize applications for maximum speed and scalability.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or a related field.
  • Proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frontend frameworks (e.g., React, Vue.js).
  • Experience with responsive design and web accessibility standards.

3. Marketing Specialist

Responsibilities:

  • Develop and optimize machine learning algorithms for image generation.
  • Manage social media accounts and create engaging content to grow our audience.
  • Analyze marketing performance metrics and optimize campaigns accordingly.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Communications, or a related field.
  • Strong writing and communication skills.
  • Experience with digital marketing tools and analytics platforms.
  • A passion for creativity and technology.

How to Apply

If you’re interested in joining our team, please send your resume and a cover letter detailing your experience and why you would be a great fit for iblackAI to contact@text2image.info. Please include the job title you are applying for in the subject line.

Equal Opportunity Employer

iBlackAI is an equal opportunity employer. We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all employees. We encourage applicants from all backgrounds to apply and join our dynamic team.
