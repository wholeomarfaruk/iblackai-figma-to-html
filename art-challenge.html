Art Challenge

iBlack AI Art Challenge Rules

Objective:

Unleash your creativity using iBlack AI's image generation platform to create stunning art pieces. Share your work and stand a chance to win exciting prizes!

Challenge Theme:

"Music Turned into Colors - AI visualizing sound"

Eligibility:

  • Open to all users of the iBlack AI platform.
  • Participants must have an active account with iBlack AI.

Submission Guidelines:

  • Use iBlack AI to generate your artwork based on the challenge theme.
  • Save and download your final image.
  • Submit your entry by posting your artwork on Bluesky, and our Discord's art challenge section using the hashtag #iBlackAIChallenge.
  • Include a short description or story behind your artwork in the post.

Judging Criteria:

  • Creativity and originality
  • Relevance to the theme
  • Artistic quality and execution
  • Engagement (likes, shares, comments)

Timeline:

  • Challenge Start Date: 2/7/2025
  • Submission Deadline: 2/9/2025 - 8PM EST.
  • Winner Announcement: 2/10/2025 - 8PM EST.

Prizes:

  • AI Vibes Suite Plan (1 month Free) Winner Takes All

General Rules:

  • Participants can submit 1 entry.
  • All entries must be original and created using iBlack AI.
  • By entering, participants grant iBlack AI the right to use their submissions for promotional purposes.
  • The decision of the judges is final.

Additional Information:

  • For any queries, contact us at challenge@iblackai.com.
  • Follow us on Bluesky and our Discord for updates and announcements.
